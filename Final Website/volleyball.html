Positive COVID-19 case forces Temperance Bedford to forfeit volleyball regional final vs. Skyline

By Greg Wickliffe |gwickliffe@mlive.com

ANN ARBOR – Ann Arbor Skyline’s volleyball squad has advanced to the Division 1 quarterfinals after Temperance Bedford forfeited the regional title game due to a positive COVID-19 case Thursday. The two teams were scheduled to face off at 7 p.m. Thursday night at Dexter for the regional championship, but a positive test from someone close to the Bedford program was confirmed earlier on Thursday, forcing the team to forfeit.

Its co-founders were Byron Shewman, a former Men’s National Team player, and Kim Oden, a former Olympian. But the goal of the small club wasn’t to attract the region’s top talent.
